# Glass Effect Discipline

Glass effect must be subtle and controlled.

Allowed:

- backdrop-blur-md
- bg-white/5 to bg-white/10
- border-white/10
- subtle gradient overlays

Not allowed:

- Heavy blur stacking
- Strong glow effects
- Neon shadows
- Overlapping blur layers

Glass only used for:
- Panels
- Floating modals
- Transport bar
